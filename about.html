About Me!

I am Yara Crystal,a Brazillian jewellery designer.I am always been curious and love to see and explore the world, learn other languages and cultures. Travelling is one of the things I enjoy the most, not only to relax, but also to learn and my research. When younger, I moved to Malaysia to study design at Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM) then to Italy to specialize in Fashion Design at Politecnico di Milano.

How I Got Into this Profession?

I was fascinated by the beauty of colors and its combinations, the mixture of geometric and organic shapes, different materials with its textures and finishes. Since I was a child, beautiful jewellerys caught my attention. I always was impressed by my mom's jewelry and from my childhood I tried to make jewellery with paper or board. Now I am working with various type of materials for making jewellery for various cultural people.

Why Me?

I do not make jewellery for mass production , rather I want to make it unique and special for individual people. I work with precision and in jewellery design only precision matters. Look how beautifully I curved this diamond ring which I made specially for a client.
